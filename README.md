# CraftLife

CraftLife is a Minecraft-themed RPG habit tracker desktop application built with Python and PyQt6. It transforms daily productivity into an engaging adventure by combining habit tracking with RPG mechanics.

## Features
- **Habits & Dailies**  
  Track your daily habits and recurring tasks.

- **Quest System (Todos)**  
  Complete quests and objectives to gain rewards.

- **RPG Stats & Progression**  
  Level up your character and improve your personal stats.

- **Shop System**  
  Spend earned rewards on in-app items and upgrades.

- **Pets & Companions**  
  Unlock and manage pets to accompany your journey.

- **Party & Boss Battles**  
  Form a party and challenge boss battles through consistent productivity.

- **Avatar Customization**  
  Personalize your in-game character.

## Built With
- Python
- PyQt6
- SQLite

## Purpose
CraftLife was created to make self-improvement more motivating and enjoyable by blending productivity tools with game-like progression inspired by Minecraft-style RPG systems.
